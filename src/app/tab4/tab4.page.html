Privacy & Security

Welcome to the Tab 4 - Privacy & Security Center

We value your trust. Here's how we protect your inventory information and maintain your privacy in our system

Key highlights:
  • Data Security: All data operations are secured with HTTPS protocols.
  • User Privacy: We don’t store personal identifiers. No login info is required.
  • Data Access: Only the inventory items you create or update are managed by you, Admin we have access over your data if needed help
  • Updates: When you edit an item, the old information is replaced immediately — we don't keep version history.
  • Deletion: Items can be deleted, except protected records like "Laptop".
  • Data Logs:We don't log your actions or share your inventory activities with anyone. However a log will be generated on your end to keep a track
  • Storage: The backend database is sorted on trusted SCU database
  • Legal: Your data will be shared to authorities investigating, with a proper legal documentated way.
  • SCU: SCU, the admin, can access and repair the data through an automated system to perserve the data integrity.
